HOW TO MAKE

To make Karahi Gosht, heat oil, saut√© two onions until golden, then add garlic and 500g meat.
Cook until browned, then add four chopped tomatoes. Stir in 2 tbsp Karahi Masala, 1 tsp red chili powder, and salt.
Cook until the meat is tender (40-60 minutes for mutton, 20-30 for chicken).
Add green chilies, cook briefly, and finish with lemon juice.
Serve hot with naan or roti
